About Me

My name is Caitlin White and I am originally from Enfield, Connecticut. I moved to Hollywood, Florida last year and currently work at Total Wine as a Wine Sales Associate. I have a degree in Performance Theatre from Western Connecticut State University, however I am looking forward to furthering my education and starting a career in the tech field!

I wrote my first line of code in seventh grade on Myspace! I learned how to make text bold, italic and underlined, and learned how to change the color of my profile. I also took CS100 at Western Connecticut State University where I built my own website! However, I wanted to gain greater knowledge and actually become employable as a software engineer!

I have three pet rats named Donnie, Cookie and Mac who I love very much, and I can't wait to make a website for them using my newly acquired skills!
